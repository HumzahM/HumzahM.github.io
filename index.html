Markets in "Event Time" Research (Oct 2023 to Ma 2024)

I worked on a project studying the markets in "event time". Right now we think about markets in calendar time - Today and Tomorrow, January and February, etc. The project asked what if we looked at it in terms of market activity? How do we split 30 years (360 calendar months) into 360 periods of equal event time? I had to build a model to figure out what that means to setup the market in terms of market activity. I ended up doing a front and back rolling average of number of trades, which was challenging since the number of trades has exponentially increased over time. The results were generally interesting but unfortunately not groundbreaking enough to be worth publishing.

What I learned: Python, Pandas, and big data related work. That I really enjoy research. That messy code leads to bad results and confusion and on my next project I need to have more discipline from day 1.
